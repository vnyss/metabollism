The AI

An assistant that actually knows nutrition

Ask it anything — macros, meal timing, gut health, sports nutrition, or why you are always hungry at 3 pm. It answers like a nutritionist who has read everything and has infinite patience.

Contextual Memory

Remembers your goals, restrictions, and history throughout the conversation. No need to re-explain yourself every message.

Meal Plan Builder

Detects when you want a full plan and asks two quick intake questions before generating a complete, usable week of meals.

Evidence Grounded

Every answer is anchored in nutrition science. It will tell you when evidence is weak or contested — honesty is part of the service.

Voice Input

Speak your question directly. Useful when you are cooking, shopping, or just do not feel like typing at the end of a long day.

Personalisation

Not a template. A plan built for you.

Generic diet plans fail because generic bodies do not exist. Too Good asks about your lifestyle, food preferences, schedule, and goal — then builds something you can actually follow.

Goal Targeting

Fat loss, muscle gain, clean eating, maintenance — your goal shapes every macro ratio and food choice the AI suggests.

Dietary Styles

Omnivore, vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or anything in between. The AI adapts without compromising your nutritional targets.

Flexible Plans

Do not like a meal? Ask to swap it. Want more protein? Ask. The plan is a living document, not a printout.

No Extremes

Too Good never recommends crash diets, extreme deficits, or supplements with questionable evidence. Sustainable is the default.
