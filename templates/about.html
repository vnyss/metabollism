FAQ

Questions we get a lot

Is this actual medical advice?

No. Too Good provides general nutrition information and personalised meal planning suggestions. It is not a substitute for a registered dietitian, doctor, or any healthcare professional. For any health condition, please consult a qualified clinician.

Is it free?

Yes. Too Good is free to use. Create an account, start a conversation, and build as many meal plans as you need. There are no hidden tiers or premium paywalls at this time.

How accurate are the plans?

Very good for general planning, but not perfect. AI-generated plans are based on established nutritional principles, but individual responses to diet vary. Use them as a strong starting point and adjust based on how your body responds.

Can I use it if I have a food allergy?

Yes. Mention your allergy clearly when starting a conversation. The AI will factor it into every suggestion. That said, always double-check ingredients for serious allergies — no AI is infallible.

What happens to my data?

Your conversation history is stored in our database only to power your experience. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it to train AI models. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.
