Why Too Good?

Most diet apps give you generic advice written for the average person. You are not average. Too Good was built to give you a plan that actually fits your body, your goals, and your life.

How It Works

Tell us your goal — lose weight, build muscle, eat cleaner, or simply feel better. Our AI reads global nutrition data and builds a meal plan around what you actually need, not a template from 2009.

Built on Real Data

Our AI is trained on nutrition research, dietary guidelines, and meal patterns from across the globe. Every suggestion is grounded in evidence, not trends.

Private by Design

Your health data belongs to you. We store only what is necessary to power your experience, and we never sell or share it. Your body, your records.

A Note from the Creator

Too Good started as a personal project — a frustration with diet tools that felt cold, confusing, and one-size-fits-all. The goal was simple: build something that actually talks to you, understands your goals, and helps you get there without the noise. This is that tool.
